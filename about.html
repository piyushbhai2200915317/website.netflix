About Netflix

Netflix is one of the world's leading entertainment services, offering a vast library of TV series, films, documentaries, and games across a wide variety of genres and languages1 . With 283 million paid memberships in over 190 countries, Netflix brings stories to life for viewers around the globe1 .

Our Mission

At Netflix, we believe in the power of stories to move us, make us feel more emotion, see new perspectives, and bring us closer to each other2 . Our mission is to entertain the world, giving our members access to the best-in-class entertainment, whenever and wherever they want it, with just one simple subscription2 .

Our History

Netflix was founded by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph with the idea to rent DVDs by mail2 . After successfully mailing a DVD to themselves without it getting damaged, the concept for Netflix was born2 . Since then, we've evolved into a streaming giant, constantly innovating to provide the best entertainment experience.

Our Commitment

We are committed to sustainability and raising awareness through our films and series2 . We also strive to create a unique and amazing employee culture, ensuring that our team members are happy and motivated to bring the best stories to our audience.
